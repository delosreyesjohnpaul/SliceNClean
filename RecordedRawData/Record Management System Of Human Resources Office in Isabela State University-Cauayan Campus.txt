The study entitled "Record Management System Of Human Resources Office in Isabela State University-Cauayan City" helps the admin input employee's data. The work of the staff will be reduced and likewise the queue time of the employees that want the service of the office.

The main purpose of this study is to develop and implement the proposed research entitled "Record Management System of Human Resources Office in Isabela State University-Cauayan City" Mainly the study aimed to: determine the listed problems
